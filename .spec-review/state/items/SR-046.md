## Acceptance criteria
- When Counterspell successfully cancels a cast, the slot/spell resources of the original caster are released automatically (no manual DM cleanup required).
- Test: caster A casts Fireball → caster B counterspells successfully → caster A's 3rd-level slot is refunded; Fireball does not resolve.
- Test: failed counterspell → original cast resolves and slot stays spent.
- Tests pass; `make cover-check` green.
